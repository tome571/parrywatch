Why some matches are set aside: two legitimate things inflate the parry counter. Counterspell lets the parry button block abilities that arrive with a loud on-screen warning — those parries almost never miss, so they say nothing about melee-parry skill. Apollo's Riposte adds "successes" to the counter without the parry button being pressed at all. Matches with either are shown in color but excluded from the verdict — the stamp is based only on what's left, where high numbers have no innocent explanation.
